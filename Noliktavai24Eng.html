Boltless system

BITO boltless storage systems will satisfy any werehouses needs. BITO boltless storage system includes quick complectation, lightness and quick movement. Galvanized finish will provide endurance against corrosion.

Boltless systems main functions:


• excellent load capacity and performance;
• can be used in public spaces – shops, offices;
• great fit in both home and industrial conditions;
• indestructible thanks to steel strength and zinc finish;
• structural components made of certified tensile steel;
• cost-effective solution.

Shelves

Solid shelves with shelf clips - our shelves feature box-profiled edges at the long sides and triple edge bending at the short sides. Reinforcing lengthwise beads and pressure joined corners ensure excellent stability.

All shelves are perforated to accommodate shelf dividers.

Home

Boltless storage systems shelves combines simplicity, high-capacity and easy assembly. That's why the will be useful for any household, independently from its residents and their needs.

Garage

Any organized garage needs high quality shelves, not only for tools, but also for tyres. Boltless shelving system offers special complectation, for storing car tyres.

Storage
